About

We are a passionate team dedicated to delivering outstanding services to our clients. Our mission is to create value and build strong relationships.

We offer a wide variety of services related to electricity, security, communication, computers, and building access. We are experts in installing, fixing, and keeping these important systems working well, making sure they are safe and meet industry standards. We work with things like electrical wiring, backup power systems (UPS), different types of security cameras (solar, internet-connected, and night vision), fire alarms, office phone systems, computer networks, printers, internet connections, fingerprint scanners for entry, and smart locks for doors. We value our customers and their safety, and we strive to provide dependable and effective solutions that meet their specific needs.

Services

1. Access Control

Biometric

This involves installing and maintaining access control systems that utilize biometric data (fingerprints, facial recognition, iris scans) to authenticate individuals.

Smart Door Lock

This focuses on the installation and maintenance of advanced door locking systems that offer features like keyless entry, remote access, and integration with other smart home devices.

2. Electrical

Electrical

This encompasses the full range of electrical work, including the installation of wiring, lighting, outlets, circuit breakers, and power distribution systems.

UPS

This involves the installation and maintenance of Uninterruptible Power Supply (UPS) systems that provide backup power during outages.

3. IT & Computing

Computer

This covers the installation and maintenance of computers, including hardware setup, operating system installation, and software configuration.

Internet

This involves setting up and maintaining internet connections, including the installation and configuration of routers, modems, and network cables.

Printer

This focuses on the installation and maintenance of printers, including driver installation, network configuration, and troubleshooting printing issues.

4. Security & Surveillance

Fire Alarm

This involves the installation and maintenance of fire alarm systems, including detectors, control panels, and alarm devices.

IP Camera

This involves the installation and maintenance of Internet Protocol (IP) cameras for high-definition video surveillance.

IR Camera

This involves the installation and maintenance of Infrared (IR) cameras for night vision surveillance.

Solar Camera

This involves the installation and maintenance of solar-powered surveillance cameras for remote or off-grid locations.

5. Telecommunications

EPBX

This involves the installation, configuration, and maintenance of Electronic Private Branch Exchange (EPBX) systems for communication within organizations.
